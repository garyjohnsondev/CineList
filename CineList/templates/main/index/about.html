What is CineList?


First and foremost, CineList is a movie sharing website. The primary function of CineList is the library.

When you create an account, you then have the ability to build out your digital library. This library is the digital representation of the movies you own physical copies of.

Once you have built your library, you then have the option to search for your friends that are also using CineList.

Once you've built up your library and friends list, you can proceed to borrow movies freely between each other.

CineList is designed to expedite and simplify the process of sharing movies with friends.

CineList makes use of the TMDb API, and may in the future take advantage of Google's API as well.
